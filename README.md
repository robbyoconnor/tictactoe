Tic Tac Toe written in Ruby

**Requires Ruby 2.2 or better.**

 To Run
ruby bin/ttt.rb

Terminology for the diagnals is taken
from Linear Algebra's Matrix terminology.

Major Diagnal: [0, 4, 8]
Minor Diagnal is [2,4,6].

     0 | 1 | 2
     --- --- ---
     3 | 4 | 5
     --- --- ---
     6 | 7 | 8
